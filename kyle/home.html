    About

    Born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, Kyle grew up surrounded by technology. His father worked as a programmer while his mother taught English. As a child, Kyle played many sports, eventually specializing in swimming during high school, where he also joined the advanced choir.

    At age 16, Kyle decided to start his own debate team in 2008. By 2011, under his coaching and organization, the team had grown to ten members and hosted multiple tournaments without faculty support. Kyle also won a number of speaking awards during his tenure before handing the team to a new teacher who was willing take over as coach.

    Kyle recieved a Bachelor's degree in Political Science and Philosophy. Since his undergraduate studies, Kyle attended UC Davis School of Law for a semester before deciding to pursue development instead. He will be getting married in July 2016, and hopes to take his skills to a company in San Francisco with a commitment to excellence and social good.
