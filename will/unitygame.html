The Adventure of Ted the Cup

The Adventure of Ted the Cup

by Will Peacock

« Made in Unity game engine »

Controls

WASD - Move Player

Spacebar - Jump

Hold Down Left Click On Mouse - Fire Coffee

Horizontal Mouse Movement - Rotate Player

Vertical Mouse Movement - Aim Coffee Up and Down

Hold Down Right Click On Mouse - Free Cam Mode (buggy)

Updates

You die less fast and turn faster

cool cool

Credits

The game was developed with Unity's 3D game engine. The songs are "Bushwick Tarantella Loop" and "Batty McFaddin" by Kevin MacLeod of incompetech.com. All 3D models were custom made in Blender. The cup model was based off of tutor4u's tutorial. The human model was based off of Sebastian Lague's tutorial. Some of the textures might be copyrighted, idk.
